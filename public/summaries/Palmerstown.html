Welcome to Palmerstown, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Palmerstown, or Baile Phámar in Irish, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It was once a part of the Pale, a fortified area around Dublin. The area has evolved over the centuries, with the addition of the Grand Canal and the development of the Palmerstown House Estate.

Attractions

From the historic Palmerstown House to the beautiful Grand Canal, there's plenty to see in Palmerstown. Don't miss the local Stewarts sports and leisure complex, a favourite among locals.

Restaurants & Pubs

For a pint and a chat, head to the local Palmerstown House. If you're after a bite to eat, try the Silver Spoon, a local favourite for its hearty Irish fare.

Green Spaces

Palmerstown is blessed with plenty of green spaces. The Waterstown Park is a local gem, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a picnic. The Grand Canal also offers a scenic route for walkers and cyclists.

Local Life

Life in Palmerstown is a blend of suburban tranquillity and city convenience. The community is tight-knit and friendly, with a strong sense of local pride.

Schools

Palmerstown boasts several excellent primary and secondary schools, including St. Lorcans Boys National School and Palmerstown Community School.

House Prices

House prices in Palmerstown are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with a mix of older homes and new developments.

Rent Prices

Rents in Palmerstown are typically lower than the Dublin average. Expect to pay around €1,200 for a 1-bed, €1,500 for a 2-bed, and €1,800 for a 3-bed. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Palmerstown is generally a safe area, with a crime index lower than the Dublin average. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Palmerstown is well-served by bus routes, and the city centre is just a short drive away. The area is also very walkable, with plenty of cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Palmerstown House was once the home of the Guinness family? Or that the area is said to be haunted by the ghost of a 17th-century highwayman?

Property Trends & Future Developments

Palmerstown is an area on the rise, with new developments and amenities adding to its appeal. Yet it retains its charming, village-like atmosphere.

Local Events & Markets

From the annual Palmerstown Festival to the weekly farmers' market, there's always something happening in Palmerstown.

Welcome to Palmerstown, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Palmerstown, or Baile Phámar in Irish, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It was once a part of the Pale, a fortified area around Dublin. Over the years, it has evolved from a rural village to a bustling suburban neighbourhood, while still retaining its historical charm.

Attractions

From the historic Palmerstown House to the beautiful Liffey Valley, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the local art gallery, and be sure to take a stroll along the Grand Canal.

Restaurants & Pubs

For a bite to eat, try the local favourite, The Silver Granite. If you're after a pint, The Manor Pub is a must-visit. Locals also recommend the Indian restaurant, Spice of India.

Green Spaces

Palmerstown is blessed with plenty of green spaces. Waterstown Park is perfect for a picnic, while the local golf course is a hit with sports enthusiasts.

Local Life

Life in Palmerstown is a blend of suburban tranquillity and city convenience. The community is tight-knit, friendly, and proud of their local heritage.

Schools

There are several primary and secondary schools in the area, including the highly-regarded Palmerstown Community School.

House Prices

House prices in Palmerstown are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with a mix of traditional and modern homes available.

Rent Prices

Rents for a 1-bed, 2-bed, and 3-bed places are typically €1,200, €1,500, and €1,800 respectively. Compared to the Dublin average, Palmerstown is quite affordable. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Palmerstown is a safe neighbourhood with a low crime index. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

With regular bus services and close proximity to the M50, commuting is a breeze. The area is also very walkable, and there are plenty of cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Palmerstown was once home to the famous writer James Joyce? And don't forget to visit the secret garden at the end of Oakcourt Avenue!

Property Trends & Future Developments

Palmerstown is a stable neighbourhood with a steady property market. There are plans for further development of local amenities and green spaces.

Local Events & Markets

From the annual Palmerstown Festival to the weekly farmers' market, there's always something happening in the neighbourhood.

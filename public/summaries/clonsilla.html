Welcome to Clonsilla, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Clonsilla, a suburb on the north-west side of Dublin, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It was once a small village, but has grown into a bustling suburb while still retaining its historical charm.

Attractions

From the historic Clonsilla Church to the beautiful Royal Canal, there's plenty to see and do in Clonsilla. Don't miss the Luttrellstown Castle, a stunning 15th-century castle with beautiful gardens.

Restaurants & Pubs

Clonsilla boasts a variety of local eateries and pubs. The Bell Pub is a local favourite, known for its friendly atmosphere and delicious food. For a more upscale dining experience, try the Castleknock Hotel.

Green Spaces

Clonsilla is home to several beautiful parks and green spaces. The Royal Canal Way is a popular spot for walking and cycling, and the Millennium Park offers a great space for family picnics.

Local Life

Life in Clonsilla is a blend of suburban comfort and city convenience. The community is friendly and welcoming, with a strong sense of local pride.

Schools

Clonsilla offers a range of primary and secondary schools, including the highly regarded Scoil Choilm Community National School and Castleknock Community College.

House Prices

House prices in Clonsilla are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with a mix of detached, semi-detached, and terraced houses available.

Commuting & Transport

Clonsilla is well-served by public transport, with regular bus and train services to Dublin city centre. The area is also very walkable, with plenty of cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Clonsilla is full of hidden gems and local legends. Be sure to visit the 'Fairy Tree' in Millennium Park, a local landmark believed to be a meeting place for fairies.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Clonsilla is a popular area for families and young professionals, with property prices steadily increasing over the past few years. The area is expected to continue to grow and develop in the coming years.

Local Events & Markets

Clonsilla hosts a range of local events and markets, including the popular Clonsilla Farmers Market and the annual Clonsilla Festival.

Welcome to Inchicore, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Once a rural village, Inchicore has evolved into a bustling urban neighbourhood with a rich history. It's home to the Royal Canal and the Irish railway industry, with the old railway works still standing as a testament to the area's industrial past.

Attractions

From the Kilmainham Gaol Museum to the Irish Museum of Modern Art, Inchicore is packed with cultural landmarks. Don't miss a stroll along the Grand Canal, or a visit to the Phoenix Park, one of Europe's largest city parks.

Restaurants & Pubs

Whether you're after a hearty Irish stew at The Black Lion or a pint at The Patriot's Inn, Inchicore's pubs and restaurants have you covered. Locals recommend the fish and chips at The Village Inn.

Green Spaces

With the War Memorial Gardens and Phoenix Park on its doorstep, Inchicore is a haven for nature lovers. The Grand Canal offers a scenic walk, and there's always a game on at the local GAA club.

Local Life

Inchicore is a vibrant, close-knit community with a strong sense of identity. It's a place where neighbours know each other by name, and the local GAA club is the heart of the community.

Schools

There are several primary and secondary schools in Inchicore, including the well-regarded St. Michael's CBS. The area is also home to the Inchicore College of Further Education.

Commuting & Transport

With its own Luas stop and several bus routes, Inchicore is well connected to the rest of Dublin. The city centre is just a 15-minute drive away, and the area is also popular with cyclists thanks to its network of cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Inchicore was the birthplace of famous Irish playwright Sean O'Casey? Or that the area is home to one of Dublin's oldest bowling clubs? Discover these and more local secrets when you explore Inchicore.

Property Trends & Future Developments

With new developments on the horizon and a growing popularity among young professionals, Inchicore is definitely an area on the rise. But don't worry, it's not losing its unique charm anytime soon.

Local Events & Markets

From the annual Inchicore Sports and Social Club's Christmas Fair to the weekly farmers' market, there's always something happening in Inchicore. And don't forget the local GAA matches, a true community event.

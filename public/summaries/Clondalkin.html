Welcome to Clondalkin: A Local's Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Clondalkin has a rich history dating back to the 7th century, with the iconic Round Tower standing as a testament to its early Christian heritage. The area has evolved over the centuries, with a mix of old and new that gives it a unique charm.

Attractions

From the Round Tower Visitor Centre to the Happy Pear café, there's plenty to see and do in Clondalkin. Don't miss the chance to explore Corkagh Park and its fairy forest!

Restaurants & Pubs

Clondalkin is home to some fantastic eateries and pubs. Locals recommend The Laurels for a pint and The Waterside for a bite to eat.

Green Spaces

With Corkagh Park and the Grand Canal Greenway, Clondalkin offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities and nature walks.

Local Life

Life in Clondalkin is a blend of village charm and city convenience. The community is tight-knit and friendly, with a strong sense of local pride.

Schools

Clondalkin boasts a number of quality primary and secondary schools, including Scoil Íde and Coláiste Bríde.

House Prices

House prices in Clondalkin are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with a mix of property types available.

Rent Prices

Rent prices in Clondalkin are typically lower than the Dublin average. Expect to pay around €1,200 for a 1-bed, €1,500 for a 2-bed, and €1,800 for a 3-bed. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Clondalkin is generally a safe area, with a crime index lower than the Dublin average. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Clondalkin is well-served by public transport, with the Luas Red Line, several bus routes, and cycleways. The city centre is a 30-minute commute away.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the fairy forest in Corkagh Park to the legendary ghost of Drimnagh Castle, Clondalkin is full of surprises and stories.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Clondalkin is an area on the rise, with new developments and regeneration projects adding to its appeal.

Local Events & Markets

Clondalkin hosts regular community events, including the annual Clondalkin Village Festival and a weekly farmers' market.

Area Vibe Score

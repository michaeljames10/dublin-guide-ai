Welcome to Ballyfermot: A Local's Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Founded in the 12th century, Ballyfermot has a rich history. From its early days as a Norman manor to its growth during the 20th century, this area has seen it all. Don't miss the Ballyfermot Heritage Group for a deep dive into local history.

Attractions

From the Ballyfermot Leisure Centre to the Cherry Orchard Equine Centre, there's plenty to see and do. And don't forget to check out the local street art!

Restaurants & Pubs

For a pint and a chat, head to The Black Forge Inn. If you're peckish, try the local favourite, Ballyfermot Cafe.

Green Spaces

Markievicz Park and California Hills Park are perfect for a stroll or a picnic. For a longer walk, try the Grand Canal Way.

Local Life

Ballyfermot is a tight-knit community with a friendly vibe. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive.

Schools

There are several primary and secondary schools in the area, including St. Louise's Primary School and Ballyfermot College of Further Education.

House Prices

House prices in Ballyfermot are generally lower than the Dublin average, making it a great place for first-time buyers.

Rent Prices

Rents are also more affordable here. Expect to pay around €1,200 for a 1-bed, €1,500 for a 2-bed, and €1,800 for a 3-bed. Compared to the Dublin average, Ballyfermot scores a solid 7/10 on the affordability scale.

Crime Index

With a safety rating of 6/10, Ballyfermot is a relatively safe area to live.

Commuting & Transport

With several bus routes and cycleways, getting around is easy. The city centre is just a 20-minute bus ride away.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Ballyfermot was the birthplace of U2's Larry Mullen Jr.? And don't miss the annual Ballyfermot Festival, a local tradition since 1988.

Property Trends & Future Developments

With new developments and regeneration projects, Ballyfermot is an area on the rise.

Local Events & Markets

From the weekly farmers market to the annual Ballyfermot Festival, there's always something happening here.

Area Vibe Score

Overall Vibe: A friendly, community-focused area with a rich history and a bright future.

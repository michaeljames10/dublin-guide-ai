Welcome to Kilmainham, Dublin's Historic Heart

History

From the ancient Kilmainham Gaol to the modern Irish Museum of Modern Art, Kilmainham's history is as rich as a pint of Guinness. It's seen rebellions, revolutions, and the birth of a nation.

Attractions

Visit the haunting Kilmainham Gaol, stroll through the stunning gardens of the Royal Hospital, or soak up some culture at the Irish Museum of Modern Art. There's never a dull moment in Kilmainham.

Restaurants & Pubs

From the hearty Irish fare at The Patriot's Inn to the craft beers at The Royal Oak, Kilmainham's food and drink scene is as vibrant as a Dublin sunset.

Green Spaces

With the Phoenix Park on its doorstep and the leafy War Memorial Gardens nearby, Kilmainham is a green oasis in the heart of the city.

Local Life

Kilmainham is a tight-knit community with a laid-back vibe. It's a place where neighbours know each other's names and the local pub is a second home.

Schools

From the highly-rated St. James's Primary School to the innovative Digital Hub, Kilmainham offers excellent educational opportunities for all ages.

House Prices

With its historic charm and central location, Kilmainham is a sought-after area. Expect to pay a premium for the privilege of calling it home.

Commuting & Transport

With the Luas Red Line, numerous bus routes, and plenty of cycle lanes, getting around Kilmainham is a breeze. And it's just a short stroll to the city centre.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the secret garden at the Royal Hospital to the legendary gigs at The Royal Oak, Kilmainham is full of surprises. And did you know that U2 recorded their first album here?

Property Trends & Future Developments

With new developments like the Digital Hub, Kilmainham is a neighbourhood on the up. But it's also fiercely proud of its heritage, so don't expect it to lose its charm anytime soon.

Local Events & Markets

From the weekly farmers' market at the Royal Hospital to the annual Kilmainham Arts Festival, there's always something happening in Kilmainham.

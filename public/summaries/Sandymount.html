Welcome to Sandymount: Dublin's Seaside Gem

History

Once a quiet fishing village, Sandymount has evolved into a bustling, vibrant neighbourhood. It's steeped in history, with landmarks like the Martello Tower and Sandymount Green telling tales of its past.

Attractions

From the scenic Sandymount Strand to the historic Joyce Tower, there's plenty to see. Don't miss the annual Bloomsday celebrations, a nod to the area's literary heritage.

Restaurants & Pubs

Whether you're after a cosy pub like O'Reilly's or a gourmet meal at Brown's, Sandymount has you covered. Locals swear by the seafood chowder at Sandymount House.

Green Spaces

Enjoy a stroll along the beach, a picnic in Herbert Park, or a nature walk in Irishtown Nature Park. The area is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts.

Local Life

Sandymount is a tight-knit community with a village feel. It's a place where neighbours know each other, and local businesses thrive.

Schools

Home to top-rated schools like Star of the Sea and Sandymount Park Educate Together, it's a great place for families.

House Prices

With its desirable location and community vibe, house prices in Sandymount tend to be higher than the Dublin average.

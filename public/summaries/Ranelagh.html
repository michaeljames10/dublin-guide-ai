Welcome to Ranelagh, Dublin's Vibrant Village!

History

Once a rural village, Ranelagh has evolved into a bustling urban neighbourhood. It's rich in history, with Georgian and Victorian architecture, and a heritage that includes the famous Ranelagh Gardens, a popular 18th-century pleasure garden.

Attractions

From the iconic Ranelagh Gardens to the bustling Ranelagh Village, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the local art galleries and the annual Ranelagh Arts Festival.

Restaurants & Pubs

Ranelagh is a foodie's paradise, with a wide range of eateries and pubs. Locals recommend the tapas at La Bodega and a pint at Birchalls.

Green Spaces

Despite its urban setting, Ranelagh is home to several green spaces. Ranelagh Gardens Park is a local favourite for a leisurely stroll or a picnic.

Local Life

Ranelagh has a vibrant community vibe, with a mix of families, young professionals, and students. It's a lively, friendly neighbourhood with a strong sense of community.

Schools

Ranelagh boasts several excellent primary and secondary schools, including Ranelagh Multi-Denominational School and Gonzaga College.

House Prices

House prices in Ranelagh are generally higher than the Dublin average, reflecting the area's popularity and amenities.

Commuting & Transport

Ranelagh is well-served by public transport, including the Luas Green Line. It's also a great area for cycling and walking, with the city centre just a short commute away.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the secret garden at Ranelagh School to the legendary Birchalls pub, Ranelagh is full of surprises. And did you know that James Joyce once lived here?

Property Trends & Future Developments

Ranelagh continues to be a sought-after area, with property prices reflecting its popularity. The area is also seeing some exciting new developments, including new eateries and shops.

Local Events & Markets

Ranelagh hosts a range of community events, from the annual Ranelagh Arts Festival to the weekly farmers' market. And don't miss the local GAA matches!

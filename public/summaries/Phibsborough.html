Welcome to Phibsborough: A Local's Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Phibsborough, also known as Phibsboro, is a vibrant neighbourhood with a rich history. It was once a rural village, but it has evolved into a bustling urban area. The area is home to many historical buildings, including the Royal Canal Bank, the former home of George Russell, a famous Irish writer.

Attractions

Phibsborough is home to many attractions, including the National Botanic Gardens, Glasnevin Cemetery, and Dalymount Park, the home of Bohemian FC. The area also boasts a variety of unique shops and boutiques.

Restaurants & Pubs

Phibsborough is a foodie's paradise, with a wide range of restaurants and pubs. Locals recommend Woodstock Cafe for a hearty breakfast, and The Back Page for a pint and a game of pool.

Green Spaces

The area is blessed with several green spaces, including the Blessington Street Basin, a hidden gem that's perfect for a peaceful stroll. The Royal Canal Way is also a popular spot for walking and cycling.

Local Life

Life in Phibsborough is vibrant and community-oriented. The area has a strong sense of community, with regular events and a friendly, welcoming vibe.

Schools

Phibsborough is home to several primary and secondary schools, including the well-regarded St. Peter's National School and Phibsborough Montessori School.

House Prices

House prices in Phibsborough are generally on par with the Dublin average. The area offers a mix of period homes and modern apartments.

Rent Prices

Rent prices in Phibsborough are slightly above the Dublin average. A 1-bed apartment typically rents for around €1,500 per month, while a 3-bed house can cost upwards of €2,500 per month. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Phibsborough is generally a safe area, with a crime index below the Dublin average. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Phibsborough is well-served by public transport, with several bus routes and the Luas Green Line nearby. The area is also very walkable, with most amenities within a short stroll.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Phibsborough is full of hidden gems and local legends. The area is home to the Bohemian FC, one of the oldest football clubs in Ireland, and the Phizzfest, a popular community arts festival.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Phibsborough is an up-and-coming area, with several new developments planned. The area is also home to many period properties, adding to its charm and character.

Local Events & Markets

Phibsborough hosts several local events and markets, including the Phizzfest and the Phibsborough Village Market.

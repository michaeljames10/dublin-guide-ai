Welcome to Howth, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Howth has a rich history dating back to the Viking era. It's home to the 15th-century Howth Castle and the 19th-century Martello Tower. The area has evolved from a small fishing village to a bustling suburb, while still retaining its historical charm.

Attractions

From the stunning Howth Cliff Walk to the Baily Lighthouse, there's plenty to see. Don't miss the Howth Market for local crafts and food, and the National Transport Museum for a bit of history.

Restaurants & Pubs

Howth is a foodie's paradise. Try the fresh seafood at Crabby Jo's, enjoy a pint at The Abbey Tavern, or indulge in gourmet dining at King Sitric.

Green Spaces

Howth is blessed with beautiful green spaces. Explore the Howth Cliff Walk, visit the Deer Park, or enjoy a picnic at Claremont Beach.

Local Life

Life in Howth is laid-back and friendly. The community is tight-knit, with regular events and a strong sense of local pride. It's a place where everyone knows your name.

Schools

Howth has several excellent schools, including Howth Primary School and Sutton Park School. The area is also close to Dublin City University.

House Prices

House prices in Howth are higher than the Dublin average, reflecting the area's desirability. Expect to pay a premium for sea views and period properties.

Rent Prices

Rents in Howth are also above average. Expect to pay around €1,500 for a 1-bed, €2,000 for a 2-bed, and €2,500+ for a 3-bed. Affordability compared to the Dublin average: 💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Howth is generally safe, with a crime index lower than the Dublin average. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Howth is well-served by public transport, with regular DART and bus services. The area is also popular with cyclists and walkers, thanks to its scenic routes.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Discover the secret garden at Howth Castle, hear the legend of the Pirate Queen at Ireland's Eye, or find the hidden seals at the harbour.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Howth is a sought-after area with stable property values. Future developments include the restoration of Howth Castle and improvements to local amenities.

Local Events & Markets

From the weekly Howth Market to the annual Prawn Festival, there's always something happening. The area also has a strong sporting tradition, with sailing and golf popular activities.

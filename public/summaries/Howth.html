Welcome to Howth, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Howth has a rich history dating back to the Viking era. It's home to Howth Castle, a structure that has evolved over the centuries, and the iconic Howth Lighthouse. The area has retained its old-world charm while embracing modernity.

Attractions

From the stunning Howth Cliff Walk to the bustling Howth Market, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the National Transport Museum and the beautiful Ireland's Eye island.

Restaurants & Pubs

Howth is a foodie's paradise. Try the fresh seafood at Crabby Jo's, enjoy a pint at The Abbey Tavern, or indulge in gourmet dining at King Sitric.

Green Spaces

Howth is blessed with abundant green spaces. Explore the Balscadden Bay, take a stroll in Deer Park, or enjoy a picnic at Claremont Beach.

Local Life

Life in Howth is laid-back and friendly. The community is tight-knit, and the area has a distinct seaside village vibe despite being just a stone's throw from Dublin city centre.

Schools

Howth has several excellent primary and secondary schools, including Howth Primary School and Sutton Park School. The area is also close to several universities and colleges in Dublin.

House Prices

House prices in Howth are generally higher than the Dublin average, reflecting the area's desirability and excellent amenities.

Commuting & Transport

Howth is well-served by public transport, with regular DART and bus services. The area is also very walkable and has several cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the secret smuggler's tunnel at the Cliff Path Loop to the legendary pirate queen Grace O'Malley, Howth is full of surprises and stories.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Howth continues to be a sought-after residential area, with property values steadily increasing. The area is also seeing some exciting new developments, including the restoration of Howth Castle.

Local Events & Markets

Howth hosts a variety of events throughout the year, including the Howth Prawn Festival and the weekly Howth Market. The area is also known for its vibrant music scene.

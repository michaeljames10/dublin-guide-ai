Welcome to Terenure, Dublin's Charming Suburban Gem

History

Once a quiet rural village, Terenure has evolved into a bustling suburban neighbourhood with a rich history. It's home to some of Dublin's most beautiful Victorian and Edwardian houses, a testament to its past as a wealthy merchant's retreat.

Attractions

From the iconic Terenure College to the bustling Terenure Village Market, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the annual Terenure Classic Car Show, a local favourite!

Restaurants & Pubs

Whether you're after a hearty Irish stew at Vaughan's or a pint at The Two Sisters, Terenure's food and drink scene won't disappoint. Locals recommend the Sunday roast at The Eaton Square.

Green Spaces

Enjoy a leisurely stroll in Bushy Park, or take the kids to the playground in Terenure Village. The area is also home to several sports clubs, including Terenure Rugby Club.

Local Life

Terenure is a friendly, community-oriented neighbourhood with a relaxed vibe. It's a great place to raise a family, with plenty of schools, parks, and local amenities.

Schools

There are several excellent schools in the area, including Terenure College, a prestigious all-boys secondary school, and Presentation Primary School, known for its strong community spirit.

House Prices

House prices in Terenure are generally above the Dublin average, reflecting the area's popularity and high quality of life. Expect to pay a premium for period properties.

Commuting & Transport

With regular buses to the city centre and easy access to the M50, commuting from Terenure is a breeze. The area is also very walkable, with most amenities within a short stroll.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Bram Stoker, author of Dracula, once lived in Terenure? And don't miss the secret garden in Bushy Park - a true hidden gem!

Property Trends & Future Developments

While Terenure has retained its historic charm, it's also embracing the future with new developments and a growing food and drink scene.

From the weekly Terenure Village Market to the annual Christmas lights switch-on, there's always something happening in Terenure.

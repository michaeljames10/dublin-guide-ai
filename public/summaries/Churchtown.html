Welcome to Churchtown: A Charming Dublin Suburb with a Rich History and Vibrant Local Life

History

Churchtown's history dates back to the 13th century, with its name derived from the old church tower that still stands today. Over the centuries, it has evolved from a rural village to a bustling suburb, retaining its unique charm and heritage.

Attractions

From the historic Churchtown Castle to the bustling Dundrum Town Centre, there's plenty to see and do in Churchtown. Don't miss the local farmers' market and the quirky Bottle Tower pub!

Restaurants & Pubs

Churchtown boasts a variety of eateries and pubs. Locals recommend the cosy Churchtown Stores for a pint and the gourmet delights at The Glenside.

Green Spaces

Enjoy the outdoors at the beautiful Marlay Park or take a leisurely stroll along the Dodder River. The area is also home to several golf clubs for sports enthusiasts.

Local Life

Churchtown has a friendly, community-oriented vibe. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive. The area is known for its family-friendly atmosphere and active community groups.

Schools

Churchtown is home to several excellent primary and secondary schools, including the highly-regarded Notre Dame School and De La Salle College.

House Prices

House prices in Churchtown are generally on par with the Dublin average, with a mix of charming period homes and modern developments.

Rent Prices

Rents in Churchtown are slightly above the Dublin average, with typical rents for 1-bed, 2-bed, and 3-bed places at €1,500, €1,800, and €2,200 respectively. Affordability rating: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Churchtown is considered a safe area with a low crime index. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Churchtown is well-served by public transport, with regular Luas and bus services. It's also a great area for cycling and walking, with plenty of cycleways and pedestrian-friendly streets.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Churchtown was once home to the famous writer James Joyce? And don't miss the secret garden at the back of the Bottle Tower pub!

Property Trends & Future Developments

Churchtown is a sought-after area that continues to attract families and professionals alike. With ongoing regeneration projects, it's an area that's constantly evolving while maintaining its unique charm.

Local Events & Markets

From the weekly farmers' market to the annual Marlay Park concerts, there's always something happening in Churchtown.

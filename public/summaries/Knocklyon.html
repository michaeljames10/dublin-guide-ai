Welcome to Knocklyon: A Hidden Gem in Dublin's Southside

History

Knocklyon, a suburban paradise, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It was once a rural area, home to the famous Knocklyon Castle. Today, it's a bustling neighbourhood with a blend of old-world charm and modern amenities.

Attractions

Knocklyon is home to the stunning St. Colmcille's Church, a must-visit. For shopping enthusiasts, Knocklyon Shopping Centre offers a variety of shops and services.

Restaurants & Pubs

From the cosy Knocklyon Inn to the delicious Italian cuisine at Bellagio, there's something for every palate. Don't miss out on the traditional Irish breakfast at The Happy Pear!

Green Spaces

Knocklyon boasts several green spaces, including the beautiful Dodder Valley Park. It's a haven for nature lovers and fitness enthusiasts alike.

Local Life

Knocklyon is a friendly, community-oriented neighbourhood. It's a place where neighbours know each other, and local events are a regular occurrence.

Schools

Knocklyon is home to several excellent schools, including St. Colmcille's Community School and Knocklyon Community School.

House Prices

House prices in Knocklyon are generally on par with the Dublin average, with a mix of semi-detached houses and modern apartments.

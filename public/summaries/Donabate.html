Welcome to Donabate: A Charming Village with a Rich History and Vibrant Community

History

Donabate, a quaint village located on the peninsula of North County Dublin, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It's home to several historic buildings, including the magnificent Donabate House and the St. Patrick's Church.

Attractions

From the stunning Newbridge House and Farm to the five local golf clubs, there's plenty to see and do in Donabate. Don't miss the beautiful beaches and the Martello Tower, a historic coastal fort.

Restaurants & Pubs

Enjoy a pint at the local pub, Keeling's, or dine at one of the many restaurants in the village. Locals recommend the Italian cuisine at Pasta Castello and the fresh seafood at Fish Shop.

Green Spaces

Donabate is known for its beautiful parks and outdoor spaces. Enjoy a walk along the coastal path, a picnic in the park, or a round of golf on one of the local courses.

Local Life

Life in Donabate is peaceful and community-oriented. The village has a friendly, welcoming vibe and a strong sense of community spirit.

Schools

Donabate has several excellent primary and secondary schools, including Donabate Portrane Educate Together and St. Patrick's Boys National School.

House Prices

House prices in Donabate are generally lower than the Dublin average, making it an attractive option for families and first-time buyers.

Commuting & Transport

Donabate is well-served by public transport, with regular bus and train services to Dublin city centre. The village is also a popular location for cycling and walking.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Donabate is home to several hidden gems, including the beautiful Turvey Nature Reserve and the historic Corballis House. The village is also known for its local legends, including the ghost of Newbridge House.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Donabate is a growing area with several new housing developments planned for the future. However, the village retains its charming, rural character.

Local Events & Markets

Donabate hosts a variety of local events and markets, including the annual Donabate Portrane Summer Festival and the weekly farmers' market.

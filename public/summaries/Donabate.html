Welcome to Donabate: A Charming Village with a Rich History and Vibrant Community

History

Donabate, a quaint village in North County Dublin, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It's home to several historic buildings, including the magnificent Donabate House and the charming St. Patrick's Church.

Attractions

From the stunning Newbridge House and Farm to the five local golf clubs, there's plenty to see and do in Donabate. Don't miss a walk along the beautiful Donabate Beach!

Restaurants & Pubs

For a bite to eat, locals recommend Keeling's Pub for its hearty fare and friendly atmosphere. For a more upscale dining experience, try the Shoreline Hotel's restaurant with its stunning sea views.

Green Spaces

Donabate is blessed with an abundance of green spaces. The Newbridge Demesne Regional Park is a local favourite, offering walking trails, a playground, and a working farm.

Local Life

Life in Donabate is peaceful and community-oriented. The village has a friendly, welcoming vibe and a strong sense of community spirit.

Schools

Donabate boasts several excellent primary and secondary schools, including Donabate Portrane Educate Together and St. Patrick's Boys National School.

House Prices

House prices in Donabate are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with a mix of charming period homes and modern developments.

Rent Prices

Rent prices in Donabate are relatively affordable compared to the Dublin average. Expect to pay around €1,200 for a 1-bed, €1,500 for a 2-bed, and €1,800 for a 3-bed. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Donabate is a safe and secure area with a low crime index. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Donabate is well-served by public transport, with regular bus and train services to Dublin city centre. The village is also very walkable and has several cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

One of Donabate's best-kept secrets is the Turvey Nature Reserve, a haven for wildlife and a great spot for a peaceful walk. And don't forget to look out for the famous Donabate Ghost!

Property Trends & Future Developments

Donabate is a growing area with several new housing developments planned. However, the village retains its charming, rural character.

Local Events & Markets

Donabate hosts a number of community events throughout the year, including the popular Donabate Portrane Summer Festival and the weekly farmers' market.

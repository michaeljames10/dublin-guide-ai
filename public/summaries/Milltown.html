Welcome to Milltown, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Milltown's history is as rich as a pint of Guinness. Once a bustling mill village, it's now a charming residential area with a unique blend of old and new. The Dodder River, which powered the mills, still flows through the heart of the area.

Attractions

From the historic Nine Arches Bridge to the modern Wilde and Green cafÃ©, there's plenty to see and do in Milltown. Don't miss the tranquil walks along the Dodder River.

Restaurants & Pubs

For a bite to eat, locals recommend The Dropping Well, a historic pub with a hearty menu. For a pint, you can't beat The Milltown House, a traditional Irish pub with a friendly atmosphere.

Green Spaces

Milltown is blessed with green spaces. The Dodder Riverbank Walk is a local favourite, and the nearby Dartry Park is perfect for a picnic.

Local Life

Life in Milltown is laid-back and friendly. It's a close-knit community with a village feel, despite being just a stone's throw from Dublin city centre.

Schools

Milltown is home to several excellent schools, including Alexandra College, a prestigious all-girls school, and Gonzaga College, a top-rated boys' school.

House Prices

House prices in Milltown are on the higher end, reflecting the area's desirability and proximity to the city centre.

Commuting & Transport

Milltown is well-served by public transport, with regular Luas and bus services. It's also a great area for cycling and walking, with plenty of cycleways and pedestrian paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Milltown was once home to a famous ghost? The White Lady of Milltown was a local legend, said to haunt the old mill. Today, the only spirits you'll find are in the local pubs!

Property Trends & Future Developments

Milltown is a stable area with a strong sense of community. While there are some new developments, the area retains its historic charm and village feel.

Local Events & Markets

From the annual Milltown Festival to the weekly farmers' market, there's always something happening in Milltown.

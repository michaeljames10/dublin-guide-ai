Welcome to Ashtown: A Charming Blend of History, Culture, and Modern Living

History

Ashtown's history is as rich as the Guinness poured in its pubs. From its humble beginnings as a small farming village, it has evolved into a vibrant neighbourhood, while still retaining its historical charm.

Attractions

From the Royal Canal to the Phoenix Park, Ashtown is brimming with attractions. Don't miss the Ashtown Castle, a restored medieval tower house that's a hidden gem.

Restaurants & Pubs

Ashtown is a foodie's paradise. From the Halfway House, known for its traditional Irish fare, to the trendy eateries along the canal, there's something for every palate. And let's not forget the cosy pubs!

Green Spaces

With Phoenix Park on its doorstep, Ashtown is a haven for nature lovers. The park is perfect for a leisurely stroll, a picnic, or even a visit to the Dublin Zoo.

Local Life

Ashtown is a friendly, welcoming neighbourhood with a strong sense of community. It's a place where neighbours know each other by name and local businesses are supported.

Schools

Ashtown boasts several excellent primary and secondary schools, making it a great place for families. The area is also home to a few notable institutions, including the Dublin City University.

House Prices

House prices in Ashtown are generally on par with the Dublin average. The area offers a mix of property types, from charming period homes to modern apartments.

Rent Prices

Rent prices in Ashtown are relatively affordable compared to the Dublin average. Expect to pay around €1,500 for a 1-bed, €1,800 for a 2-bed, and €2,200 for a 3-bed place. Affordability rating: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Ashtown is a safe neighbourhood with a low crime index. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Ashtown is well-served by public transport, including the Luas, Dart, and several bus routes. The area is also very walkable and has excellent cycleways.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the legendary Ashtown Ghost to the secret garden at the back of the Halfway House, Ashtown is full of surprises. And let's not forget the annual Duck Race on the Royal Canal!

Property Trends & Future Developments

Ashtown is an up-and-coming neighbourhood with several exciting developments in the pipeline. But don't worry, it's not losing its charm anytime soon.

Local Events & Markets

From the weekly farmers' market to the annual Ashtown Festival, there's always something happening in Ashtown. The community spirit here is truly something special.

Welcome to Donaghmede, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Donaghmede, a suburb on the northside of Dublin, has a rich history dating back to the 1970s. Originally a farmland, it has evolved into a bustling residential area with a strong sense of community.

Attractions

Donaghmede Shopping Centre is a local landmark, offering a variety of shops and services. The area is also home to the beautiful St. Kevin's Church, a must-visit for architecture enthusiasts.

Restaurants & Pubs

From the traditional Irish pub, The Cock and Bull, to the popular Chinese restaurant, Golden Elephant, Donaghmede offers a variety of dining options that locals love.

Green Spaces

Donaghmede is home to several parks, including Fr. Collins Park, a perfect spot for a family picnic or a leisurely walk.

Local Life

Life in Donaghmede is peaceful and community-oriented. The area has a friendly vibe, with locals often gathering for community events.

Schools

Donaghmede boasts several primary and secondary schools, including the well-regarded Holy Trinity National School.

House Prices

House prices in Donaghmede are generally more affordable compared to other parts of Dublin, with the average price for a three-bedroom house being around €300,000.

Rent Prices

Rent prices are also relatively affordable, with a one-bedroom apartment costing around €1,200 per month, a two-bedroom apartment around €1,500, and a three-bedroom house around €1,800. Compared to the Dublin average, Donaghmede is quite affordable. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Donaghmede is generally safe, with a crime index lower than the Dublin average. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Donaghmede is well-served by public transport, with several bus routes and the nearby Howth Junction DART station. The area is also very walkable, with most amenities within a short distance.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

One of Donaghmede's hidden gems is the local community garden, a tranquil spot loved by locals. The area is also home to the legendary Dublin band, Aslan.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Donaghmede is an up-and-coming area, with several new developments planned, including a new community centre and additional green spaces.

Local Events & Markets

Donaghmede hosts several community events throughout the year, including the popular Donaghmede Festival. The area also has a weekly farmers' market, offering fresh local produce.

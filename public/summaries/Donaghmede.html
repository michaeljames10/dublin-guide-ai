Welcome to Donaghmede: A Local's Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Donaghmede, a suburb on the northside of Dublin, has a rich history dating back to the 1970s. Originally farmland, it has evolved into a bustling residential area with a strong sense of community.

Attractions

Don't miss the Donaghmede Shopping Centre, a local hub for shopping and socializing. For a bit of culture, visit the nearby Odeon Cinema or the beautiful St. Kevin's Church.

Restaurants & Pubs

For a hearty meal, locals recommend the traditional Irish fare at The Cock and Bull. If you're after a pint, The Donaghmede Inn is a friendly local pub with a great selection of beers.

Green Spaces

Donaghmede is home to several parks, including the popular Fr. Collins Park, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a family picnic.

Local Life

Life in Donaghmede is laid-back and community-focused. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive.

Schools

Donaghmede boasts several excellent primary and secondary schools, including the highly-regarded Holy Trinity National School.

House Prices

House prices in Donaghmede are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with the average house price being around €300,000.

Rent Prices

Rents in Donaghmede are also relatively affordable, with a 1-bed costing around €1,200, a 2-bed €1,500, and a 3-bed €1,800. Compared to the Dublin average, Donaghmede is quite affordable. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Donaghmede is generally a safe area, with a crime index lower than the Dublin average. Safety: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Donaghmede is well-served by public transport, with several bus routes and the nearby Howth Junction DART station. The city centre is just a 20-minute drive away.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

One of Donaghmede's best-kept secrets is the beautiful St. Donagh's Church, a hidden gem tucked away in the heart of the suburb.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Donaghmede is an up-and-coming area, with several new developments planned in the coming years.

Local Events & Markets

Donaghmede hosts a weekly farmers' market, where locals gather to buy fresh produce and homemade goods.

Area Vibe Score

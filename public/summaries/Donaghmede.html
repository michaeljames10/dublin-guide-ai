Welcome to Donaghmede, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Donaghmede, a suburb on the northside of Dublin, has a rich history dating back to the 1970s. Originally a farmland, it has evolved into a bustling residential area with a strong sense of community.

Attractions

Donaghmede Shopping Centre is a local landmark, offering a variety of shops and services. The area is also home to the beautiful St. Kevin's Church, a must-visit for architecture enthusiasts.

Restaurants & Pubs

From the traditional Irish pub, The Cock and Bull, to the popular Chinese restaurant, Golden Elephant, Donaghmede offers a variety of dining options that locals love.

Green Spaces

Donaghmede is home to several parks, including Fr. Collins Park, a perfect spot for a family picnic or a leisurely walk.

Local Life

Life in Donaghmede is peaceful and community-oriented. The area has a friendly vibe, with locals often gathering for community events.

Schools

Donaghmede boasts several primary and secondary schools, including the well-regarded Holy Trinity National School.

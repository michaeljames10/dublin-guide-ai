Welcome to Cherrywood: Your Comprehensive Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Cherrywood's history is as rich as a pint of Guinness. From its early days as a rural village to its evolution into a bustling suburb, Cherrywood has always been a place of growth and change. The area is steeped in heritage, with landmarks like the 18th-century Tully Church reminding us of its past.

Attractions

From the stunning views at Cherrywood Park to the bustling Cherrywood Business Park, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the local art installations scattered throughout the area, adding a touch of creativity to every corner.

Restaurants & Pubs

Cherrywood boasts a variety of eateries and pubs. Locals recommend the hearty fare at The Cherrywood Arms and the delicious Indian cuisine at Spice of India. For a pint, you can't beat the atmosphere at The Old Punch Bowl.

Green Spaces

Cherrywood is a green oasis, with parks and nature walks aplenty. Be sure to visit the beautiful Cherrywood Park and the tranquil Tully Park for a breath of fresh air.

Local Life

Living in Cherrywood is like being part of a tight-knit community. The area has a friendly, welcoming vibe, and there's always something happening, from local sports matches to community events.

Schools

Cherrywood is home to several excellent primary and secondary schools, including St. Kevin's National School and Cabinteely Community School. The area is also close to several notable institutions, such as University College Dublin.

Commuting & Transport

Getting around Cherrywood is a breeze, thanks to the Luas, Dart, and numerous bus routes. The area is also highly walkable and boasts several cycleways. Commute times to the city centre are typically around 30 minutes.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Cherrywood is full of hidden gems and local legends. Be sure to visit the secret garden at Tully Park and keep an eye out for the famous Cherrywood Cat, a local feline who's become something of a celebrity.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Cherrywood is an up-and-coming area with several exciting developments in the pipeline, including a new town centre and additional green spaces. However, the area retains its charming, community-focused vibe.

Local Events & Markets

Cherrywood hosts a variety of local events and markets, from the weekly farmers' market to the annual Cherrywood Festival. There's always something happening in this vibrant community.

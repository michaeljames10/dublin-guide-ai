Welcome to Donnybrook, Dublin's Charming and Historic Neighbourhood

History

Donnybrook's history is as rich as a pint of Guinness. From its roots as a Viking settlement to its evolution into a bustling suburb, Donnybrook has always been a place of character and charm.

Attractions

From the historic Donnybrook Fair to the modern RTE studios, there's always something to see and do in Donnybrook. Don't miss a stroll along the Dodder River, either!

Restaurants & Pubs

Whether you're after a hearty Irish stew or a craft cocktail, Donnybrook's got you covered. Locals recommend O'Connell's for a pint and Mulberry Garden for a bite.

Green Spaces

Donnybrook is home to Herbert Park, a lush green oasis perfect for picnics, sports, or a leisurely walk. The Dodder River also offers beautiful nature trails.

Local Life

Living in Donnybrook is like being part of a tight-knit community. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive.

Schools

Donnybrook boasts excellent primary and secondary schools, including the highly-regarded Donnybrook BNS and Muckross Park College.

House Prices

House prices in Donnybrook tend to be higher than the Dublin average, reflecting the area's desirability and excellent amenities.

Commuting & Transport

Donnybrook is well-served by public transport, including the Luas and Dart. It's also a great area for cycling and walking, with the city centre just a short commute away.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the legendary Donnybrook Fair to the secret garden at the Royal Hospital, Donnybrook is full of surprises. And did you know that James Joyce once lived here?

Property Trends & Future Developments

Donnybrook is a stable and sought-after area, with property values expected to hold steady. The area is also seeing some exciting new developments, including a revitalised village centre.

Local Events & Markets

Donnybrook is a hub of community activity, with regular events like the Donnybrook Fair and a popular weekly farmers' market.

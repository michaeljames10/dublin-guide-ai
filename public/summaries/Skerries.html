Welcome to Skerries, Dublin's Seaside Gem

History

Once a bustling fishing port, Skerries has evolved into a charming seaside town with a rich history. From the 5th century monastic settlement on Church Island to the 19th century Skerries Mills, the town's heritage is as diverse as it is fascinating.

Attractions

From the iconic Skerries Mills to the beautiful beaches, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the stunning views from Red Island or the chance to spot seals at the harbour!

Restaurants & Pubs

Seafood lovers will be in heaven at Stoop Your Head, while the Brick House offers a cosy spot for a pint. For a true local experience, don't miss Ollie's Place.

Green Spaces

Enjoy a stroll along the South Strand, explore the Ardgillan Castle grounds, or simply relax in one of the town's many parks. Outdoor enthusiasts will love the coastal walks and cycle paths.

Local Life

Skerries is a close-knit community with a laid-back seaside vibe. It's a place where everyone knows your name and there's always a friendly face to greet you.

Schools

With a number of highly-rated primary and secondary schools, Skerries is a great place for families. The local community college is also highly regarded.

House Prices

House prices in Skerries tend to be higher than the Dublin average, reflecting the area's popularity and coastal location.

Rent Prices

Rents for a 1-bed start around €1,200, while a 3-bed can set you back €2,000 or more. Compared to the Dublin average, Skerries is moderately affordable. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Skerries is generally a safe area with a low crime index. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

With regular DART and bus services, getting around is easy. The town is also very walkable and there are plenty of cycle paths.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

From the annual Skerries Midsummer Festival to the legendary story of St. Patrick's footprint, there's always something interesting happening in Skerries.

Property Trends & Future Developments

While Skerries retains its charming character, there's also a buzz of development with new homes and amenities being added.

Local Events & Markets

From the weekly farmers market to the annual Skerries Soundwaves Festival, there's always something going on in Skerries.

Area Vibe Score

Welcome to Leopardstown: Your Local Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Leopardstown, named after a local tavern, has a rich history dating back to the 13th century. It's evolved from a quiet rural area to a bustling suburb, with a unique blend of old-world charm and modern amenities.

Attractions

From the famous Leopardstown Racecourse to the Central Park business district, there's plenty to see and do. Don't miss the stunning views from Ticknock Hill!

Restaurants & Pubs

Enjoy a pint at The Leopardstown Inn or indulge in some fine dining at China Sichuan. Locals also recommend the Italian fare at Belluccis.

Green Spaces

Leopardstown is blessed with beautiful green spaces. Enjoy a stroll in Cabinteely Park or explore the trails in the Dublin Mountains.

Local Life

Leopardstown offers a relaxed suburban lifestyle with a friendly community vibe. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive.

Schools

Leopardstown boasts excellent schools, including St. Kilian's German School and Nord Anglia International School Dublin.

House Prices

House prices in Leopardstown are generally above the Dublin average, reflecting the area's desirability and amenities.

Leopardstown is a sought-after area with steady property values. Future developments include a new town centre and additional green spaces.

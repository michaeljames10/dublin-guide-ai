Welcome to Marino, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Marino, a small, intimate neighbourhood, has a rich history dating back to the 1920s. It's known for its unique 'garden city' architecture and the iconic Marino Casino, a stunning 18th-century neoclassical building.

Attractions

From the Marino Casino to the beautiful St. Vincent's Park, there's plenty to see. Don't miss the local GAA club, a hub of community activity and a great place to catch a hurling match.

Restaurants & Pubs

Marino might be small, but it's got some cracking spots for a bite or a pint. Locals love the cosy atmosphere at The Casino at Marino and the hearty fare at The Yacht Pub.

Green Spaces

Marino is blessed with green spaces. St. Vincent's Park is a local favourite, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a family picnic. The area is also home to several community gardens.

Local Life

Marino is a tight-knit community with a friendly, welcoming vibe. It's a quiet, residential area with a strong sense of local pride and a slower pace of life.

Schools

Marino has several excellent primary and secondary schools, including Marino College and St. Vincent's CBS. The area is also home to Marino Institute of Education, a well-regarded teacher training college.

Commuting & Transport

Marino is well-served by public transport, with several bus routes passing through the area. It's also a short walk to the Clontarf Road DART station, and the city centre is just a 15-minute cycle away.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Marino is full of surprises. Look out for the 'Fairy Tree' in St. Vincent's Park, a local landmark adorned with children's wishes. And don't forget to visit the Marino Casino, a hidden architectural gem.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Marino is a stable, mature area with a steady property market. There are no major developments planned, so the area's unique character is set to remain intact.

Local Events & Markets

From the annual Marino Fun Day to the weekly farmers' market in St. Vincent's Park, there's always something happening in Marino.

Welcome to Castleknock: Your Comprehensive Guide to Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Castleknock, a suburb with a rich history dating back to the Norman invasion, has evolved into a charming and vibrant neighbourhood. Its name, derived from the Irish Caisleán Cnucha, means 'Cnucha's Castle'.

Attractions

From the historic Castleknock Castle to the Phoenix Park, there's plenty to see. Don't miss the Farmleigh House, a government-owned estate turned public attraction.

Restaurants & Pubs

For a pint and a chat, head to The Carpenter. If you're after a bite, Myos is a local favourite, serving up hearty Irish fare.

Green Spaces

Castleknock is blessed with greenery. Phoenix Park, one of Europe's largest city parks, is right on the doorstep. It's perfect for a leisurely stroll or a family picnic.

Local Life

Castleknock is a friendly, community-oriented area with a relaxed vibe. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive.

Schools

Castleknock boasts excellent schools, including Castleknock Community College and Castleknock College, a top-ranking secondary school.

House Prices

House prices in Castleknock tend to be above the Dublin average, reflecting the area's desirability and excellent amenities.

Rent Prices

Rents are also on the higher side. Expect to pay around €1,500 for a 1-bed, €1,800 for a 2-bed, and €2,200 for a 3-bed. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Castleknock is a safe neighbourhood with a low crime index. Safety rating: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Castleknock is well-served by public transport, including the Luas and Dart. It's also a great area for cycling and walking, with plenty of cycleways and pedestrian-friendly streets.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Bram Stoker, author of Dracula, was a Castleknock resident? And don't miss the secret garden in Phoenix Park - a true hidden gem!

Property Trends & Future Developments

Castleknock continues to be a sought-after area, with new developments enhancing its appeal while preserving its unique character.

Local Events & Markets

From the annual Castleknock Festival to the weekly farmers' market, there's always something happening in Castleknock.

Welcome to Artane, Dublin's Hidden Gem

History

Artane, or Ard Aidhin in Irish, has a rich history dating back to the 12th century. It was once a rural village, but has evolved into a bustling suburb with a unique blend of old and new.

Attractions

Artane is home to the beautiful St. David's Park, the historic Artane Castle, and the Artane School of Music, which is famous for its boys' band.

Restaurants & Pubs

From the cosy Artane House Pub to the delicious Chinese food at the Golden Wok, there's something for every palate in Artane.

Green Spaces

Artane boasts several green spaces, including Rockfield Park and St. David's Park, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a family picnic.

Local Life

Artane has a friendly, community-oriented vibe. It's a place where neighbours know each other and local businesses thrive.

Schools

Artane is home to several excellent schools, including St. David's Boys National School and Artane Montessori Circle.

House Prices

House prices in Artane are generally more affordable than in central Dublin, with the average house price being around €350,000.

Rent Prices

Rent prices in Artane are also relatively affordable, with a 1-bed costing around €1,200, a 2-bed €1,500, and a 3-bed €1,800. Compared to the Dublin average, Artane is quite affordable. Affordability: 💸💸💸💸💸⚪⚪⚪⚪⚪

Crime Index

Artane is generally a safe area, with a crime index lower than the Dublin average. Safety: 🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒🔒⚪⚪⚪⚪

Commuting & Transport

Artane is well-served by public transport, with several bus routes and close proximity to the Harmonstown DART station. It's also a very walkable area, with most amenities within a short stroll.

Hidden Gems & Local Legends

Did you know that Artane was the birthplace of famous Irish musician Phil Lynott? And don't miss the hidden gem that is the Artane Market, a treasure trove of vintage finds.

Property Trends & Future Developments

Artane is a stable and mature area, with a steady property market. However, there are exciting new developments on the horizon, including a proposed new park.

Local Events & Markets

Artane hosts a variety of local events, from the annual Artane Festival to the weekly farmers' market at the Artane Castle Shopping Centre.

Area Vibe Score
